Christmas Letter

DearMyMom,
I just want to take a moment to say thank you—for everything. Words cannot fully express how grateful I am for all the love, care, and sacrifices you’ve made for me. You’ve been my rock, my cheerleader, and my biggest source of inspiration.

Thank you for always being there, no matter what. For your hugs when I needed comfort, your wisdom when I felt lost, and your patience even when I made mistakes. I know that everything you do comes from a place of love, and I want you to know that I see it and cherish it deeply.

You’ve taught me the values of kindness, resilience, and hard work, and I carry those lessons with me every day. I’m so lucky to have you as my mom, and I hope you know how much I love and appreciate you.

I promise to do my best to make you proud and to give back even just a fraction of the love and care you’ve given me. Thank you for being you—the most amazing mom anyone could ask for.

With all my love,
Kurt Jarren Mirasol❤️
